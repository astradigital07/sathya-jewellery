Years of Heritage
Our Heritage

A Legacy Forged in Gold & Devotion

Founded in 1985 in the temple town of Madurai, Sathya Jewellery began as a humble family workshop crafting ceremonial pieces for the region's most revered deities. What started as an act of devotion slowly blossomed into a name trusted by families across South India for their most treasured celebrations.

Three generations of master karigars have poured their craft into every ornament that leaves our atelier — each piece carrying the weight of tradition and the lightness of contemporary design. We believe jewellery is not merely adornment; it is memory made tangible, love cast in gold, and heritage passed from one hand to the next.
